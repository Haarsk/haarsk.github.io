  • Tint: Green/Magenta correction (-50/+50).
  • Presets: 2700K, 3200K, 4300K, 5600K, 6500K.

5. FX Tab

32 Effects (Cinema & Shnopia Abstract).

  • Param A: Structure / Zoom.
  • Param B: Variation / Color.
  • Param C: Dynamic / Threshold.
  • Color Override: Force your own colors onto effects via the top palette.

6. MATRIX Tab

  • Presets: Standard ratios (1:1, 16:9, Titan...).
  • Custom: Manual X/Y definition.
  • Mods: Multiplier, Warp (Liquid distortion), Noise (Grain).

7. TEXT Tab

  • Scrolling text, font choice, speed, color.
  • Effects: Neon, Glitch, Wave, Mirror, Breathe...
